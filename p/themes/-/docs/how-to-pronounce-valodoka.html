How to Pronounce Valodoka

Have you ever wondered how to pronounce our name? It's a fun word, but we get that it can be tricky!

The correct pronunciation is: **va-low-DO-ka**

Here's a simple breakdown:

  • **va** - rhymes with "ma" or "pa"
  • **low** - rhymes with "go"
  • **doh** - rhymes with "go" but with a strong emphasis!
  • **ka** - rhymes with "ma" or "pa"

Say it all together: **va-low-doh-ka**
